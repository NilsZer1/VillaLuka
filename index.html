Experience Villa Luka, an exquisite retreat
in Zadar. This haven offers an outdoor pool,
fitness center, garden, and complimentary
WiFi with mesmerizing sea views. The villa
features four bedrooms, a well-equipped
kitchen, and multiple bathrooms. Towels and
linens are thoughtfully provided. The 24-hour
front desk ensures your needs are met, and a
barbecue area and playground await. Suitably
near attractions like Kornati Yacht Marina and
Biograd Heritage Museum, Villa Luka offers
comfort and convenience. Zadar Airport is only
6 km away. But there's more to this story of
indulgence and relaxation. As you step onto
the sun-kissed grounds of Villa Luka, you'll
find yourself immersed in a world where every
detail has been artfully designed to create
an ambiance of unparalleled luxury. The outdoor
pool beckons with its crystal-clear waters,
inviting you to take a refreshing dip or simply
lounge by the water's edge, letting the cares of
the world slip away.
